Quality Assurance

Our commitment to excellence in every adhesion promoter product

Our Quality Standards

Ensuring consistency and performance in every chemical formulation

ISO 9001:2015 Certified Manufacturing

Our quality management system is certified to international standards, ensuring consistent quality in all our adhesion promoter formulations and manufacturing processes. Every batch meets stringent quality requirements for printing inks and paints applications.

Advanced Chemical Analysis

Every product undergoes comprehensive chemical analysis including color stability testing, viscosity measurement, adhesion strength evaluation, and heat resistance verification using state-of-the-art laboratory equipment.

Statistical Process Control

We employ statistical process control methods to monitor critical parameters like titanium content, pH levels, and viscosity throughout production runs, ensuring batch-to-batch consistency.

Expert Quality Team

Our quality assurance team consists of trained chemical engineers and laboratory technicians with specialized expertise in adhesion promoter chemistry and quality control methodologies.

Quality Control Process

Our comprehensive three-stage quality verification system

Raw Material Inspection

All incoming chemicals including titanium compounds, organic chelates, and solvents are verified for purity, composition, and material certifications before entering our production process.

In-Process Monitoring

Critical parameters such as reaction temperature, mixing time, and chemical composition are continuously monitored during formulation to ensure optimal product characteristics.

Final Product Testing

Completed adhesion promoters undergo comprehensive testing for color stability, viscosity, adhesion performance, and heat resistance before packaging and shipment to customers.

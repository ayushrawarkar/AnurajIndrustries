About Anuraj Industries

Leading manufacturer of adhesion promoters for printing inks & paints since 2016

Our Story

Anuraj Industries initiated its business activities in 2016 with the sole aim of providing quality Adhesion Promoter for Printing Inks & Paints. Working with the best team and utilizing advanced technology, we have acquired a position as a reputed manufacturer, exporter and supplier of specialized chemical solutions.

We have established ourselves as a leading supplier of IAC-10 (Titanium Phosphate Complex), Titanium Acetyl Acetonate (TAA), and Titanium Ammonium Lactate (LA) chelate for inks and paints. Our products are embedded with features like superior appearance, color stability, optimal viscosity, excellent adhesion, and resistance to heat.

Our Mission

To provide innovative adhesion promoter solutions for the printing inks and paints industry through advanced chemical technology, skilled workforce, and unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the preferred partner for adhesion promoter chemicals by continuously improving our formulations, investing in research & development, and expanding our global reach in the printing and coating industry.

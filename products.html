IAC-10 (Titanium Phosphate Complex)

IAC-10 is a Titanium Phosphate Complex that provides excellent crosslinking and superior adhesion to various substrates under heat and solvent conditions.

It is used as an adhesion promoter for printing inks & paints.

Specifications:

  • Appearance: Clear to slightly yellow liquid
  • Solubility: Soluble in organic solvents
  • Active Content: Approx. 50%
  • Specific Gravity: 1.2 - 1.3
  • Viscosity: 100 - 300 cps

Applications:

  • Printing Inks
  • Paints & Coatings
  • Wire Enamels
  • Adhesives

Titanium Acetyl Acetonate (TAA)

Titanium Acetylacetonates are available in multiple grades as yellow to red liquid chelates with varying percentages of active ingredient.

Specifications:

  • Appearance: Yellow to red liquid
  • Solubility: Soluble in organic solvents
  • Active Content: 50-80% (depending on grade)
  • Specific Gravity: 1.1 - 1.3
  • Viscosity: 50 - 500 cps

Applications:

  • Catalyst for esterification and polymerization
  • Crosslinking agent
  • Adhesion promoter
  • Surface modifier

Titanium Ammonium Lactate (LA) Chelate

Hydrolytically stable, chelated titanium solution in clear to light yellow aqueous liquid with approximately 50% active content.

Specifications:

  • Appearance: Clear to light yellow liquid
  • Solubility: Water soluble
  • Active Content: Approx. 50%
  • pH: 6.0 - 8.0
  • Specific Gravity: 1.2 - 1.3

Applications:

  • Water-based systems
  • Paper coatings
  • Textile treatments
  • Adhesion promotion
